# Financial Performance Dashboard
### Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze financial performance across products, customer segments, and countries using Tableau to uncover sales trends, profitability drivers, and regional revenue insights.

## Dataset Overview
- The dataset covers sales transactions from September 2013 to December 2014.
- It includes information related to products, customer segments, countries, sales revenue, and profit.
- Date columns were used to enable monthly time-based trend analysis.
- Data spans 5 countries: Canada, France, Germany, Mexico, and the United States.

## Tools Used
- Tableau Desktop
- Excel / CSV (data source)

## Dashboard Overview

- The dashboard is built across multiple sheets combined into a single interactive view, with filters for Segment and Country. It includes the following visualisations:
- KPI Cards — Total Sales (£118.73M), Total Profit (£16.89M), and Profit Margin (14.23%).
- Sales Trend — Monthly line chart tracking revenue from September 2013 to December 2014.
- Profit by Product — Horizontal bar chart ranking products by total profit.
- Sales by Segment — Bar chart comparing revenue across Government, Small Business, Enterprise, and Midmarket segments.
- Sales by Country — Bar chart comparing revenue across the five countries in the dataset.

## Key Findings

- Total sales reached £118.73M with a profit of £16.89M and an overall margin of 14.23%.
- Paseo was the most profitable product, generating over £4.79M in profit.
- Government was the highest-revenue segment at £52.5M, followed by Small Business at £42.4M.
- Sales peaked in December 2014, showing strong end-of-year performance.
- Revenue across all five countries was broadly comparable, ranging from approximately £21M to £25M.
- A notable sales dip occurred in mid-2014 before recovering strongly in Q4.
